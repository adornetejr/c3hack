O Hackathon

Todas as soluções (app / webapp / game etc.) serão classificadas em duas categorias:

01

Ideia

Como sua idéia se encaixa no paradigma "aprenda | ensine | explore | inove", como você conectou tecnologia e educação para ajudar no processo de aprendizagem, etc.

02

Qualidade Técnica

Como foram boas as suas decisões técnicas (ferramentas, processos, arquitetura, frameworks etc.), como você conseguiu desenvolver sua solução durante 24 horas, se você tem ideias de como desenvolvê-la ainda mais etc.

Para ambas as categorias, concederemos um prêmio. Os prêmios são independentes um do outro. Você pode tentar ganhar ambos ou concentre-se mais em apenas um.

FAQ

  • Why should I focus on education?

    At Setapp we're trying to push the notion of meaningful software. Software that not only works but also makes a difference. We feel that allowing people to learn new, complex ideas in a fun and easy way is the essence of meaningful software, at the same time providing a real challenge.

  • How can I apply?

    Before applying you need to have an idea for the project you'll be developing. While filling out the application form you'll find a few questions about your idea. We'll use these answers to select the teams that will be invited to participate in the hackathon. Once you submit your details you'll receive a confirmation e-mail. We'll contact the selected teams and invite them to take part in the hackathon.

  • How many people can be on one team?

    There can be a up to 6 people in a team. The number of people isn’t crucial. It’s important that you are a team ready for the challenge!

  • How do I get there?

    Our headquarter is a 20-minute walk from the Railway Main Station. You can get there by tram or bus (the stop is called Rondo Jeziorańskiego). If you prefer to come by car, you can park in the paid zone in City Park (we'll arrange a free entry for you). Take a look at the map for more details.

  • What kind of technologies can I use?

    At Setapp we don't restrict ourselves to any specific technologies so we won't limit what you can do. You can develop whatever solution you like: a mobile or web app, maybe an embedded solution with custom hardware. You can use VR and/or AR. But make sure that you know the technology you're going to use before starting the challenge.

  • How our projects will be assessed?

    There will be two categories – each with a prize. Not only is your idea important but also the implementation of your project. We would like to give a chance to those who not only have an idea but also put effort in the execution of their project. So if you're up for the challenge – bring an awesome idea, implement it in a perfect way and grab both prizes.

    The technical quality of your project will be judged based on many factors including: your choice of technology/frameworks, the architecture of your code, how extensible your code is etc.

  • How to follow information about the event?

    We will update information on Facebook. You can follow our event there. Crucial information will be also sent to the Team Captains' e-mail. Captains, remember - pass all the information to your team!

  • What attractions will be provided?

    You will have the possibility to play with the newest technology, such as HTC Vive, Oculus Rift, HoloLens etc. We will show you what possibilities new technologies provide. Should you feel tired after a few hours of working, there will be place to rest and boost your energy.

    And, of course, we won't let you be thirsty or hungry during the event.

Somos um membro ativo da revolução da informação. Em desenvolvimento software que importa é a nossa paixão. Projetos significativos motivam nos a trabalhar mais. Queremos que você se junte a nós e sinta isso atmosfera.
